### Here are few threats to devices and servers

- DDoS

Let me explain them inshort one by one:

**1. DDoS:**
- DDoS = Distributed Denial of Service
- It is an attack by an hacker using botnets on a web server to crash it from overload of using its max processing capacity.
- Botnets are the group of systems which are attacked being controlled by hacker using malware deployed inside their systems.
- This chaos can divert the attention of defenders while hackers steal somewhere else.
- This can also be done to make losses to any company as the server dosent allow entry of normal users.

**2. Ransomeware:**
- Ransomeware is a software that gets inside you system when someone clicks a bad link or downloads an unknown file.
- Hackers run code finding ur importatn data like pictures and demand ransom which might nt even guarantee ur files to be back.
- There was a huge attack called *"WannaCry"*(One of my favorite viruses tbh). It locked patient details in worldwide hospitals. Its flaw was found by a random person experimenting wih it and I found it quite fun.

**3. Social Engineering:**
- I find social engineering quite powerful cause humans might trust you more seeing you physically rather than on web without any acute details. Though its hard to maintain the trust.
- Social Engineering is all about how u utilise weakness of humans like, their emotions(like scammers do), their curiosity(like an uknown web link does to them), their carelessness.
- Hackers also might become one of the employees of the company they want to invade in or even use an insider by bribing them and it works rather very easily than getting inside their systems if their security is strong.
